TRAVEL TIPS

  • When scuba diving in Mississippi, don't actually go into the water; aligators can be bitches.

  • Be sure to use oxygen in your scuba tanks, not helium.

  • Always wear a home-made chum necklace when scuba diving in the Gulf Coast.

  • Regardless of the posted signs, clothing should be worn while diving around snapping turtles.

  • No matter how thirsty you are, don't consume more than one gallon of ocean water per hour.

  • Check local laws regarding fishing license requirements.
